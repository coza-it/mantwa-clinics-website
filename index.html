Who We Are

Built for the people of Kutlwanong and Thabong

Mantwa Clinics was founded on one conviction: that proximity to a city should not be a prerequisite for good healthcare. Dr. Malapa Modisane built these two practices from the ground up, and they remain deeply rooted in the communities they serve.

Our staff are not just healthcare providers. They are neighbours. They know their patients by name, understand their circumstances, and show up with genuine care every single day.

How We Work

What guides us every single day

These aren't words on a wall. They shape how we treat every patient who walks through our doors, in Kutlwanong and Thabong, every day.

01

You come first

Every call we make as a clinic starts with one question: what is best for this patient? Not what is easiest, not what is quickest.

02

We see the person, not the case

Illness is personal. We take the time to listen, understand, and respond with genuine care rather than a standard script.

03

This is our community too

Dr. Modisane and the team are part of Kutlwanong and Thabong. The health of these communities is something we take personally.

04

We do what's right

No shortcuts. No compromises. We hold ourselves to the highest professional and ethical standards, even when no one is watching.

05

Good enough is not enough

We keep learning, keep improving, and keep pushing the standard of care higher. Your health deserves nothing less.

06

Healthcare is for everyone

Cost should never be the reason someone does not get help. We keep our doors open and our rates fair, without compromise on quality.

Our Services

Comprehensive Primary Healthcare

From general consultations and preventive care to HIV clinical management and specialised health assessments, Mantwa Clinics delivers expert-led care for every patient.

General Consultations

Comprehensive medical consultations, diagnosis, and treatment for patients of all ages.

Preventive Care & Screenings

Health screenings, immunisations, and preventive wellness programmes to keep you healthy.

HIV Clinical Care

Comprehensive HIV testing, counselling, ARV initiation, and ongoing clinical management for patients living with HIV.

Specialised Healthcare Assessments

In-depth health assessments and screenings tailored to individual patient needs, identifying risks early for better outcomes.
